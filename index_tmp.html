How we met

Kyunghun first met Katie in the spring of 2017 at Bethel Korean Church. They both were carpooling together to the retreat. Kyunghun was attracted to Katie but was nervous and shy to get closer to her. Kyunghun couldn’t stop thinking about her even after the retreat. Luckily, when it was about time to get bible study groups reassigned, Kyunghun and Katie were assigned in the same group; Kyunghun was so thrilled. Finally, Kyunghun took the courage and asked Katie for a date. After spending some time together, they enjoyed getting to know each other and made sweet memories day by day. Katie felt loved and also was attracted by his kindness, faithfulness and positivity. Their love has grown deeper and deeper. After 2 years of dating, Kyunghun wanted to spend his life with her forever. Kyunghun surprised Katie by proposing at the Chesapeake Bay where he took her on their first date. Katie said “YES!!” We are so excited to start the next chapter together and can't wait to celebrate with all of you in January.
